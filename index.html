COTED Vision, Mission, and Goal

Vision

CPSU as the leading technology-driven multi-disciplinary University by 2030.

Mission

CPSU is committed to produce competent graduals who can generate and extend leading technologies in multi-disciplinary areas beneficial to the community.

Goal

1. Enhance human resource capability.
2. Provide and nurture technology-driven culture.
3. Create and deliver new, innovative technologies.
4. Establish effective, "Green" and resilient infrastructure and facilities.
